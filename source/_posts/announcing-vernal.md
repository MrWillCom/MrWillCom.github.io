Today is the vernal equinox—the date I picked for the debut of The Vernal Fortnightly. I'm so excited to introduce my brand-new personal journal to you.

As you know, my blog posts here have been a bit unpredictable, making it hard to anticipate the next post. Sometimes I even spend so much time on a single piece that something may get potentially outdated before publishing. The Vernal Fortnightly is here to fix that. By launching this newsletter, I'm committing to a consistent schedule: a new issue every two weeks, carrying my latest updates.

The fortnightly will focus on my life experience, fresh experiments, product or project updates and personal curations. I'll be gathering thoughts and fine-tuning them throughout the fortnight to ensure every issue is ready for your inbox.
